# Transform Customer Interactions with AgentForce

- adam
- November 20th, 2024

Discover how **AgentForce by Salesforce** is reshaping the way businesses engage with customers in our latest demo video. Using the fictional Coral Cloud Resorts as an example, we dive into the transformative power of generative AI and its seamless integration with Salesforce. Here’s a quick summary of what you’ll learn:

**From Chat to Generative AI**

- Trace the evolution of chat functionality from simple widgets to sophisticated AI-driven bots.
- See how AgentForce leverages natural language understanding to deliver accurate, conversational responses using Salesforce data.

**Personalised Customer Experiences**

- Watch a demo of a guest booking a relaxing experience through the chat widget.
- See how AgentForce verifies details, identifies customer preferences, and completes bookings in real time.

**Seamless Salesforce Integration**

- Go behind the scenes to see how AgentForce updates records, checks availability, and handles booking details directly in Salesforce.
