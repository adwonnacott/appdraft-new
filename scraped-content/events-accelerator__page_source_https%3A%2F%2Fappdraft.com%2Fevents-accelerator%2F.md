## Why Standard CRMs Don't Work for Events

Salesforce is a powerful platform that can transform how you run your events business. The challenge? Most CRMs - Salesforce included - are designed around straightforward sales processes: managing contacts, tracking deals through stages, closing deals. That works brilliantly if you're selling software licences or consultancy services.

But events businesses operate differently. Your "opportunity" isn't just a contact and a deal value - it's a room booking that needs to appear on a calendar, vendor coordination across multiple suppliers, complex payment schedules, setup requirements, and a dozen other moving parts that need to work together seamlessly. Standard CRM functionality simply doesn't address these specific challenges out of the box.
