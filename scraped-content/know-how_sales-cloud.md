## What is Sales Cloud?

Sales Cloud manages your entire sales lifecycle, from lead capture through to deal close. It provides the tools your team needs to stay organised and focused.

It includes:

- Management of leads, accounts, contacts and opportunities
- Task tracking, activity history, calendar and **email integration** (with Outlook or Gmail)
- Quote generation
- Forecasts and quota attainment to help you track against your revenue goals
- Dashboards and reports for user-friendly analytics
- Mobile access for teams in the field
